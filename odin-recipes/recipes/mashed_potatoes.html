Creamy Mashed Potatoes Recipe

This creamy mashed potatoes recipe is shockingly good! It is absolutely Thanksgiving and Christmas worthy. The folks at your holiday table will swoon over these whipped potatoes!

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Garlic
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Cream Cheese
  • Fine sea salt

Steps

  1. Cut the potatoes
  2. Boil the potatoes
  3. Prepare your melted butter mixture
  4. Pan-dry the potatoes
  5. Mash the potatoes
  6. Stir everything together
  7. Taste and season
